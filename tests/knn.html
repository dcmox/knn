kNN Demonstration

k-Nearest Neighbor (kNN) is a machine learning and classification algorithm used to group similar points together and make a judgement based on the data available. This is a fun little demonstration to give you an idea of the capabilities of the k-Nearest Neighbor algorithm.

In this example, we chose k=3 to find the 3 nearest neighbors to our point. With the sample dataset, we can classify based on a person's location (the red dot) that a person may be "Drunk" or "Sober" depending on the proximity of bars/clubs or schools/parks in the area.
